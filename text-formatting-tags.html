4. Text Formatting Tags

I. Headings (h1 to h6)

Explanation: Headings ka use titles ya subheadings dene ke liye hota hai. <h1> sabse bada heading hota hai aur <h6> sabse chhota.

II. Paragraph Tag

Explanation: <p> tag ka use paragraphs banane ke liye hota hai. Ye text ko alag line aur spacing ke sath dikhata hai.

III. Line Break (br) & Horizontal Line (hr)

Explanation:
<br> tag se line break aata hai bina new paragraph ke.
<hr> se ek horizontal line draw hoti hai jo sections ko separate karti hai.

IV. Bold, Italic, Underline

Explanation:
<b> ya <strong> - Bold text
<i> ya <em> - Italic text
<u> - Underlined text

V. Superscript & Subscript

Explanation:
<sup> - Superscript (upar likhne ke liye)
<sub> - Subscript (neeche likhne ke liye)
