17. CSS Flexbox: Advanced Concepts

I. Align-items & Align-self

Explanation: align-items container ke andar sabhi items ko vertically align karta hai. align-self se ek individual item ko override kiya ja sakta hai.

II. Justify-content

Explanation: justify-content horizontal alignment control karta hai flex container me.

III. Flex-grow & Flex-shrink

Explanation: flex-grow space expand karne ke liye hota hai, jabki flex-shrink space kam karne ke liye hota hai.

IV. Flex Wrap

Explanation: flex-wrap allow karta hai items ko wrap hone ka jab width kam ho.
