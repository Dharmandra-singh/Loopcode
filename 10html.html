10. Multimedia in HTML

I. Audio Tag

<audio> tag se hum web page par sound file play kar sakte hain. Common formats: MP3, OGG, WAV.

  • controls: Player dikhata hai
  • autoplay: Page load hote hi play shuru hota hai
  • loop: Audio repeat hota hai

II. Video Tag

<video> tag video play karne ke liye hoti hai. Common formats: MP4, WebM, OGG.

  • controls: Video player UI show karta hai
  • autoplay: Automatically video play karta hai
  • loop: Video repeat hota hai

III. Embedding YouTube Video

YouTube video embed karne ke liye <iframe> use hota hai.
